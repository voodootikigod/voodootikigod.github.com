JSConf Goes to Europe

Today, JSConf announced plans to take over another continent, Europe. On November 7th and 8th, 2009, the JSConf team will be presenting the first ever JSConf.eu in Berlin, Germany. This is an exciting event driven by the German contingency of the JSConf team, namely Holger, Malte, and Jan. The preliminary speaker list is up on the web site http://jsconf.eu and its stunning! We are definitely upping the ante and thats how it should be, conference after conference! Be sure to get your ticket for JSConf.eu it will sell out fast.
